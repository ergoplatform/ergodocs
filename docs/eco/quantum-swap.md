# Quantum Swap

Quantum Swap was an ErgoHack VI proposal for peer-to-peer exchange of NFTs and native assets on Ergo.

The project belongs with Ergo's broader set of non-custodial trading and escrow experiments. It should be treated as a historical ecosystem entry unless current deployment, repository, or maintainer sources are found.

## Context

ErgoHack VI focused on cypherpunk finance projects. Quantum Swap was listed as a P2P NFT and native-asset exchange service, alongside other experiments in swaps, options, privacy tooling, and decentralized infrastructure.
