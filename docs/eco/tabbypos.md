# TabbyPOS

[TabbyPOS](https://tabbylab.io) is a Web3 point-of-sale project for merchant crypto payments. Its public introduction describes a peer-to-peer payment model where crypto moves directly from the customer's wallet to the merchant's wallet, without TabbyPOS taking custody of assets.

The project lists Ergo among its integrated chains alongside ICP, Solana, Pi Network, BSC, and Aptos. The team also describes merchant payment records, multi-chain device support, Google Play availability, and a planned official market launch.
